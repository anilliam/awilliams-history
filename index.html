About me

My name is Andrew Williams. I was born in Scarborough, North Yorkshire in 1999, and I grew up just outside the town in a village called Crossgates. I went to sixth form in Sandbach, Cheshire, and from 2018–21 I attended Lancaster University and graduated with a BA (Hons) in History and Politics. I worked as a Collections Assistant at the Victoria Gallery & Museum, part of the University of Liverpool, from 2021–22, on a one-year fixed-term contract.

Since August 2022, I have been a postgraduate research student at the University of the West Indies, Mona, in Jamaica, registered for a Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in History. I am supported by a Study Abroad Studentship from the Leverhulme Trust.
