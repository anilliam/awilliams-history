Welcome to my website

I am a student at the University of the West Indies, Mona, in Jamaica, registered for a Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in History. I am supported by a Study Abroad Studentship from the Leverhulme Trust.

I am from the UK and grew up in Scarborough, North Yorkshire. I attended Lancaster University in north west England from 2018–21, and graduated with a BA (Hons) in History and Politics. I worked at the University of Liverpool from 2021–22 on a one-year fixed-term contract, in the Victoria Gallery & Museum.
